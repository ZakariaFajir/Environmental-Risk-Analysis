🌍 Environmental Risk Analysis

This tool helps you analyze environmental risks by gathering news articles, extracting key risk factors, and generating a visual risk analysis. Simply enter a topic above and click "Analyze" to get started.

🔍 How It Works:

  • Enter a topic related to the environment (e.g., Climate Change, Air Pollution).
  • Click the "Analyze" button.
  • Our system will gather news articles and identify key environmental risks.
  • You’ll receive a summary of risks, key factors, and a severity analysis.
  • A visual risk chart will help you understand the severity of different risks.

📝 Example Topics:

  • 🌎 Climate Change – Effects of global warming and mitigation efforts.
  • 🏭 Air Pollution – Industrial emissions and urban air quality.
  • 💧 Water Scarcity – Impact of droughts and water shortages.
  • 🌳 Deforestation – Environmental impact of forest destruction.

⚠️ Understanding Severity Levels

The **severity score (1-10)** indicates the impact of an environmental risk. Here's how the levels are determined:

Severity Level Impact Description Example
1-3 (Low) Localized impact, minimal risk Small oil spill in a lake
4-6 (Moderate) Regional impact, some economic/ecological effects Industrial air pollution affecting a city
7-8 (High) Nationwide impact, significant damage Widespread deforestation causing habitat loss
9-10 (Critical) Global catastrophe, irreversible effects Climate change leading to rising sea levels

📌 Note: The severity score is automatically calculated based on the **scale, frequency, and long-term effects** of the risk.
